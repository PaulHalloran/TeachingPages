I've tended to explore these in detail in one situation (e.g. what happens to air moving from the equator towards the pole, or how does the Hadley cell (the one closed to the equator) from. - What happens to water moving along the equator due to Coriolis?

- Why does the Polar cell form?

- Why does the Ferrel cell form?
